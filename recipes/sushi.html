Cucumber and avocado sushi

These rolls are easy to make and you can add either fake crab or smoked salmon.

Serve with teriyaki or soy sauce and wasabi!

Ingredients

  • 1 Â¼ cups water
  • 1 cup uncooked glutinous white rice (sushi rice)
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 4 sheets of nori (dry seaweed)
  • 1/2 Cucumber, sliced into thin strips
  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted and sliced

Steps

  1. Combine the water and rice in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Cover, reduce heat to low and simmer for 20 minutes, or until

    is tender and water has been absorbed. Remove from the heat and stir in the vinegar and a pinch of salt. Set aside to cool.

  2. Cover a bamboo sushi mat with plastic wrap to keep the rice from sticking. Place a sheet of seaweed over the plastic.

    Use your hands to spread the rice evenly onto the sheet, leaving about 1/2 inch of seaweed empty at the bottom.

    Arrange strips of cucumber and avocado across the center of the rice. Lift the mat and roll over the vegetables once and press down.

    Unroll, then roll again towards the exposed end of the seaweed sheet to make a long roll. You may moisten with a little water to help seal.

  3. Use a sharp wet knife to slice the rolls into 5 or 6 slices. Serve cut side up with your favorite sushi condiments.
